Treating search engines as editorial products with intrinsic biases can help understand the structure of information flows in new media. This paper outlines an empirical methodology to analyze the representation of topics in search engines, reducing the spatial and temporal biases in the results. As a case study, the methodology is applied to 15 popular conspiracy theories, examining type of content and ideological bias, demonstrating how this approach can inform debates in this field, specifically in relation to the representation of non-mainstream positions, the suppression of controversies and relativism.</p></div>\n\t\t<br />\n\t\t</div>\n\t\n\t\t\t<div id="articleSubject">\n\t\t<h4>Keywords</h4>\n\t\t<br />\n\t\t<div>search engines; media bias; search engine bias; media analysis; conspiracy theories; spatio-temporal bias</div>\n\t\t<br />\n\t\t</div>\n\t\n\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t<div id="articleFullText">\n\t\t<h4>Full Text:</h4>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href="http://firstmonday.org/ojs/index.php/fm/article/view/5597/4652" class="file" target="_parent">HTML</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t</div>\n\t\n\t\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<br />\n\t\t<br />\n\t\tDOI: <a id="pub-id::doi" href="https://doi.org/10.5210/fm.v20i7.5597">https://doi.org/10.5210/fm.v20i7.5597</a>\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\n\n\n<!-- start AddThis -->\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<script type="text/javascript">\n\t\t\t\tvar addthis_pub = \'sgroote@uic.edu\';\n\t\t\t</script>\n\t\t\t\t<br />\n\t\t<br />\n\t\t<div class="addthis_container">\n\t\t\t<a href="http://www.addthis.com/bookmark.php"\n\t\t\t\tonmouseover="return addthis_open(this, \'\', \'\', \'Google chemtrails: A methodology to analyze topic representation in search engine results\')"\n\t\t\t\tonmouseout="addthis_close()" onclick="return addthis_sendto()">\n\t\t\t\t\t<img src="//s7.addthis.com/static/btn/lg-share-en.gif" width="125" height="16" border="0" alt="Bookmark and Share" style="border:0;padding:0" />\n\t\t\t</a>\n\t\t\t<script type="text/javascript" src="//s7.addthis.com/js/200/addthis_widget.js"></script>\n\t\t</div>\n\t<!-- end AddThis -->\n\n\t\t\n<!-- Google Analytics -->\n<script type="text/javascript">\nvar gaJsHost = (("https:" == document.location.protocol) ?
